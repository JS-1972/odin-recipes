Roasted Vegetables

Description:

Prep: 5 minutes

Cook time: 10-30 minutes

If you're looking for a quick and healthy side dish, it's hard to beat these roasted veggies.

Goes well with just about any main dish.

Ingredients:

  • Fresh vegetables of your choice. Some good options: broccoli, cauliflower, brussels sprouts, onions, bell pepper, carrots, sweet potatoes, etc.
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, or dry seasoning of your choice

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F.
  2. Chop all vegetables into bite-size pieces.
  3. Drizzle vegetables with olive oil, use hands to make sure pieces are well coated on all sides.
  4. Arrange veggie pieces in one layer on a large baking sheet. Be careful not to crowd.
  5. Season with salt and pepper (or seasoning of your choice).
  6. Place pan in oven and cook for 10-30 minutes, or until "golden brown."
  7. Check periodically to make sure some vegetables aren't scorching before others are done. If needed, remove faster cooking vegetables from pan and set aside until all others are finished cooking.
  8. Remove pan from oven when vegetables reach the desired level of doneness.
  9. Let cool and enjoy!
