Baked Chicken

Description:

Prep: 5 minutes

Cook time: 40 minutes

This mouth-watering chicken is quick and easy to prepare, and pairs well with our roasted vegetables recipe.

Your family will love this flavorful and satisfying main dish.

Ingredients:

  • 3 to 4 medium boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • Olive oil cooking spray
  • BBQ Rub or dry seasoning of your choice

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F.
  2. Spray large baking dish or pan with cooking spray.
  3. Place chicken breasts in pan and season both sides with dry seasoning.
  4. Place pan in oven and cook for 20 minutes.
  5. Place aluminum foil over chicken breasts and cook for another 10 minutes.
  6. Remove aluminum foil covering and cook for another 10 minutes.
  7. Remove pan from oven, check internal temp of chicken (should be at least 165 degrees F).
  8. Place foil over chicken and let rest for 10 minutes.
  9. Slice chicken crossways (perpendicular to the grain).
  10. Pair with sides of your choice, and enjoy this healthy and delicious dish!
